ReliefLink – Smart Food Donation & Disaster Support Platform

📌 Project Overview

ReliefLink is a community-driven web platform designed to reduce food wastage and provide timely support to people in need. The platform connects food donors, volunteers, NGOs, and beneficiaries through a single digital ecosystem.

Users can donate surplus food, request food assistance, register as volunteers, and participate in disaster relief activities. The system ensures that excess food reaches the right people instead of being wasted while also supporting emergency response and community welfare initiatives.

🎯 Objective

- Support needy individuals and communities
- Connect donors, volunteers, and NGOs
- Assist in disaster relief and resource distribution
- Promote social responsibility through technology
